Hobbies

Basketball

Passion. Fun. Happiness.

For me, Basketball is something that I watch, play, analyze, and sometimes bet my money on. It keeps me active and helps keep my mental state in check. It's a great source of cardio for me and always puts a smile on my face. Some of my all time favorite players include Michael Jordan (GOAT), Stephen Curry, Kevin Durant, Kawhi Leonard, Shaquille O'Neal, and Lebron James (baby GOAT). Go RAPS!

Outdoors

Adventure. Peace. Excitement.

Being outdoors always gets me excited as it allows me to be spontaneous and live life to the fullest. Whether it's summer where I can enjoy playing volleyball on the beach or go hiking or whether it's winter where I can enjoy snow tubing and zip-lining. Some of the places that I love visiting include Muskoka, Tobermory, Rattlesnake Point, Mont-Tremblant, Scarborough Bluffs, and local trails.

Onewheel

Lively. Joyful. Thrilling

In case you didn't know, Onewheel is a self-balancing electric board which I absolutely cherish. It's essentially controlled by sensors which monitor your motion. I love taking it to trails and just exploring in general is extremely fun. I would likely take my onewheel over my car if I have to go nearby as it has a range of about 15 miles. Heck, I commute to my gym with this gem.
